1. How to add a shortcut on the Desktop?

    [Desktop Entry]
    Version=1.0
    Type=Application
    Terminal=false
    Exec=/usr/local/android/eclipse/eclipse -data /usr/local/android/workspace
    Name=Eclipse
    Icon=/usr/local/android/eclipse/icon.xpm
    Encoding=UTF-8
    StartupNotify=true
    Categories=Applicatio;Development;
